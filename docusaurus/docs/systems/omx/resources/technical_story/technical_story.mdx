# Technical Story

Hey there! Welcome to the **OMX Technical Story** section.

The first step in robotics begins with hands-on experience—touching the hardware and understanding the principles of movement. OMX is the ideal educational platform designed to provide beginners with the most intuitive and efficient experience in robot control.

Beyond the simple joy of assembly, you can see firsthand how fundamental principles like kinematics and inverse kinematics are translated into real-world motion. By experiencing the transformation of complex engineering theories into dynamic movements, OMX serves as a reliable partner to help you build a solid foundation in robotics.
